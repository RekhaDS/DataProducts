##App Title - Kid-centives
##This is a simple web application mainly for incentivizing the kids, i.e: to inspire their best behavior. 
## But everyone in the family participates. Each family member initially has $1 in their account. 
##For any misbehavior/mischief tickets are issued. A Ticket button click means 0.35 cents is deducted from 
## their account.  All good behavior is rewarded. With a Reward click, the total increases by 0.65 cents in their account.
